Pressure Relief Valve (PRV)
A spring-loaded safety device that opens at a preset pressure to prevent overpressure in systems. It opens when pressure goes above the set limit and closes once normal pressure resumes.
Vacuum Relief Valve (VRV)
Opens under negative internal pressure to admit air into the system, protecting against vacuum collapse.
Features & Applications
1. Bulk hauling trailers/ equipment.
2. Light gauge tanks.
3. Protection of high-volume vacuum pumps and conveying systems.
4. Stainless Steel 304 construction is available on request.
5. Available in Cast Iron Body and other parts in Gun Metal.
6. Relief Valves are available in sizes ranging from 2" to 3".
7. Protection of low to medium pressure high volume blowers, compressors and pneumatic conveying systems.
